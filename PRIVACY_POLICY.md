# Privacy Policy for Doulet AI Assistant

**Last Updated:** December 5, 2025

## Overview

The Doulet AI Assistant extension is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our extension.

## Information We Collect

### 1. What We Collect
- **Selected Text**: When you highlight text on a webpage, we temporarily access only the selected text to send it to OpenRouter's AI API for processing
- **Extension Settings**: Your preferences and settings are stored locally in your browser (chrome.storage.sync)

### 2. What We Do NOT Collect
- **No Browsing History**: We do not collect or track the websites you visit
- **No Personal Information**: We do not collect your name, email, address, or any personally identifiable information
- **No Content Storage**: We do not store the content of webpages you visit
- **No Usage Analytics**: We do not track how often you use the extension

## How We Use Information

### 1. Selected Text Processing
- The text you highlight is sent **only** to OpenRouter's API to generate AI responses
- This text is not stored by our extension
- The text is not used for any other purpose

### 2. Local Storage
- Your extension settings are stored only in your browser
- This data never leaves your device except for your OpenRouter API key (which you provide)
- Settings include model preferences, temperature, and other configuration options

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 1. OpenRouter API
- We send your selected text and API key to OpenRouter.ai for AI processing
- OpenRouter's privacy practices are governed by their own terms and privacy policy
- We do not control how OpenRouter handles your data

### 2. Third Parties
- We do **not** share, sell, or disclose your information to any third parties
- We do not use advertising or analytics services that collect your data

## Data Security

### 1. Local Storage Security
- Your settings are stored securely in your browser's local storage
- Only your OpenRouter API key is stored, which you explicitly provide

### 2. Data Transmission
- All communication with OpenRouter uses HTTPS encryption
- Your selected text is transmitted securely

## Your Rights

### 1. Control
- You control what text is sent to the AI by selecting it yourself
- You can disable the extension at any time
- You can delete your settings by removing the extension

### 2. API Key Management
- You can change or remove your OpenRouter API key at any time
- Your API key is only used to authenticate with OpenRouter's services

## Children's Privacy

- Our extension is not directed to children under 13
- We do not knowingly collect information from children

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our extension support channels.
